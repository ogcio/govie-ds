# Accordion

The Accordion component allows users to upload one or more files.  
It extends the native HTML element while allowing customization and consistent styling across your application.

<Section current="react">

<ComponentPreview>
  <Accordion>
    <AccordionItem label="What is the Citizens Information Service?">
      The Citizens Information Service provides information on public services
      and entitlements. It helps citizens access services like social welfare,
      health services, and more.
    </AccordionItem>
    <AccordionItem label="How can I apply for social welfare benefits?">
      To apply for social welfare benefits, you need to fill out an application
      form, provide necessary documentation, and submit it online or at your
      local office.
    </AccordionItem>
  </Accordion>
</ComponentPreview>
