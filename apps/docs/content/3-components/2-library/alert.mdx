## Usage

### Best Practices

### When to use this component

An alert may be a notification that keeps people informed of the status of the system and may or may not require the user to respond. Such notifications may be errors, warnings, and general updates.
This component replace the following components in local library:
- [Error Summary](https://storybook.design-system.ogcio.gov.ie/?path=/docs/typography-error-summary--docs)
- [Warning Text](https://storybook.design-system.ogcio.gov.ie/?path=/docs/typography-warning-text--docs)
- [Notification Panel](https://storybook.design-system.ogcio.gov.ie/?path=/docs/layout-notification-panel--docs)

### When not to use this component

On long forms, always include in-line validation in addition to any error messages that appear at the top of the form.
